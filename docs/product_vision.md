## 5. Habit Scope & Evolution Strategy

### The Vision: Standalone Platform
Rafeeq is designed to be a **standalone product**.
While it integrates with apps (like Prayer Times), its core engine (Accountability) is independent.

### Phase 1: The "Salah" MVP (Embedded)
*   **Context**: Built inside the Prayer Time App.
*   **Focus**: Single Habit (Salah).
*   **Goal**: Perfect the "Reflection Loop" and "Persona" with a specific use case.

### Phase 2: The "General" Platform (Standalone)
*   **Context**: A separate Rafeeq App.
*   **Focus**: Habit-Agnostic (Work, Study, Health).
*   **Goal**: Users import any schedule; Rafeeq holds them accountable.

This separation ensures that while we start niche (Muslims/Salah), the technology is built to scale to **general human productivity**.
